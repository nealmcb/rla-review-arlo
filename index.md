## About

Each review applies a common checklist based on [Arlo PR #2350](https://github.com/votingworks/arlo/pull/2350), covering artifact integrity, hash-commitment timing, sample reproducibility, risk level verification, and discrepancy analysis. Source code is in [`src/`](https://github.com/nealmcb/rla-review-arlo/tree/main/src).

The goal is to provide independently executable evidence about what each audit did and did not establish — not to certify or discredit any outcome, but to document what a member of the public can and cannot independently verify.

The Georgia May 2026 review was directed by Neal McBurnett.
